Rama and Lakshmana

Rama and Lakshmana accompnay each other almost everywhere. Even when Rama was sent into exile. His brother gladly followed his lead.

Family

Rama is one of four brothers. His father King Dasharatha had four childeren amongst his four wives. All four brother followed the morally just path of "dharma" and each of them are very loyal to their familial bonds. Rama and Lakshmana accompany each other throughout the entire epic of the Ramayana, while Bharta justly yeilds the throne to Rama at the end of the epic.
